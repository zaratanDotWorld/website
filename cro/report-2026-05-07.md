## What Is Underperforming

- **`cta_waitlist_sage` fires 0 times.** This is the primary housing conversion event — either the element isn't visible above the fold on `/houses/sage`, the copy isn't compelling, or the CTA is broken. This is the single most damaging gap in the data.
- **Homepage engagement is the lowest of major pages (0.490).** With 102 sessions — the most of any page — the home page is failing to route visitors to either funnel. There are no tracked CTA events originating from `/`.
- **Chore Wheel install completion rate is ~18%.** The funnel collapses between `/chorewheel/start` (13 sessions, `cta_chorewheel_get_started` x11) and actual install events (`cta_install_chores` x2, Hearts/Things x0). The `/chorewheel/start` page is not closing.
- **Newsletter signups are nearly zero: 3 total.** With 249 sessions and Substack identified as the primary growth channel, capturing email is a missed compounding opportunity on every page.
- **Most blog posts bounce immediately.** `/p/three-provocations` (0.273), `/p/the-story-of-chore-wheel` (0.200), `/p/the-magic-circle` (0) — entries from search or social are not staying or clicking through.

## Recommendations

### 1. Fix `cta_waitlist_sage` on `/houses/sage` — **P0**
**Page:** `/houses/sage`
**Change:** Audit why `cta_waitlist_sage` fires 0 times. Verify the event is wired correctly in `track.js`. If functional, move the interest CTA above the fold and replace any soft "learn more" language with a direct call to action: "Join the waitlist — $1,250/mo all-in." Add a second CTA anchor at the bottom of the page. The 80% application-to-move-in rate is a powerful closer — display it near the CTA.
**Expected impact:** Even lifting `cta_interest_form` from 2 to 5–8 fires/month meaningfully improves the housing pipeline. This page has the intent; it needs the conversion surface.
**Effort:** S

### 2. Add split CTAs above the fold on the homepage — **P1**
**Page:** `/`
**Change:** The homepage has 102 sessions/month and a 0.490 engagement rate — the worst of major pages. Add two explicit CTA buttons in the hero, one per business: "Find Housing at Sage House" (`cta_waitlist_sage`) and "Install Chore Wheel" (`cta_chorewheel_get_started`). Add a concrete proof line: "9 residents · 80% application-to-move-in · 6 years operating." Direct traffic (147 sessions, 0.408 engagement) is arriving with brand familiarity but no path to action.
**Expected impact:** Reduces homepage bounce; increases downstream CTA events from the highest-traffic page.
**Effort:** S

### 3. Strengthen the install CTA on `/chorewheel/start` — **P2**
**Page:** `/chorewheel/start`
**Change:** 13 sessions reach this page and only 2 `cta_install_chores` events result — the page is not closing. **Expected impact:** Lifts install conversions from the existing "get started" intent; currently ~0% of `cta_chorewheel_get_started` clicks complete an install.
**Effort:** M

**Page:** `/houses/sage`, `/chorewheel`

**Expected impact:** Compounds over time: each subscriber is a re-engageable prospect for both the housing waitlist and Chore Wheel adoption.
