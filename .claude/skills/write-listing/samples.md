# Listing samples

Reference listings for the `write-listing` skill to learn from.
Add real listings you see (ours or competitors') that do something worth imitating — or worth avoiding.

For each sample, keep the format below.
The **annotation** is the point: raw copy teaches tone, the annotation teaches judgment.

## Format

```
### <short label>
- Channel: Craigslist | SpareRoom | Listings Project | Facebook | other
- Source: <url or where you saw it>
- Why it's here: <what it does well, or the anti-pattern to avoid>

<the listing copy, verbatim>
```

### Silverlake Townhouse

- Channel: Facebook
- Source: online
- Why it's here: typical of grassroots listing (written by roommate, "looking for fit"-coded), no housing law concerns

Looking for a new housemate to join our lovely townhouse!

ABOUT THE HOUSE

We are located in the lush heart of Silverlake, walking distance from oodles of good stuff. We have a lovely backyard, great parking, and coin laundry too. The room has a big mirror closet, and central AC. Your room would be on the top floor, and you would share the bathroom with only one other housemate.

ABOUT US

We're 4 quiet creative folks in our 30's, all respectful of each others' personal space, work schedules, and keeping up with cleanliness. We love to connect & be conscious of others in the home. We are pretty damn clean we would love if you'd be willing to contribute to keeping the home shiny & clean.

ABOUT YOU

-Clean and caring to the home space

-Organized with your bills (i.e. employed)

-LGBTQ welcoming and 420 friendly

-Vegetarian/vegan/pescatarian

-No pets sorry

-No couples

-The rent for this room is $1425 per month (+ $1425 security deposit). Bills are an additional approx $70 bi-monthly (power & internet).

If you're interested please send us a message with a bit about yourself- what you do for fun, what you do for work, what are you hoping to find in a home? How do you envision living with others? Age? and your various (public) social media handles. If you seem like a good match we'll reach out to set up a showing.

Looking forward to hearing from you!

### Lightning Lofts

- Channel: WhatsApp
- Source: a community housing group
- Why it's here: Lightning Lofts is a managed coliving space run by an arts/events organization, more similar to Sage House

### Sage House

- Channel: Craigslist
- Source: online
- Why it's here: this is our latest ad, so you can see what we've been doing so far

Room in Creative Coliving House — All-Inclusive

Large room (172 sqft, first floor, one window, semi-private bath) in a fully-restored 1905 Craftsman with nine bedrooms and four full baths.

Included: utilities, WiFi, shared budget for food and supplies (~$150/mo value). No service fees. Coin-op washer/dryer, parking extra.

Shared spaces: restaurant-style kitchen, original living and dining rooms, library, recording studio, art space, gym, guest room, upstairs deck, backyard fire pit. Hardwood and tile floors throughout.

The vibe: vetted mix of creatives and professionals. Most decisions and communication happen over Slack; the house runs as a do-ocracy. One meeting and 4–5 hours of chores per month. Projects — art, music, food — encouraged. House is clean and generally quiet, with occasional dinners and parties. No pets due to allergies.

Lease: 4 month minimum then month-to-month.

Learn more and apply at zaratan.world/interest, or reply to this listing.
