This matches CO-GTM.md: the landing stage of the funnel is doing its job; the known leak is downstream (interest → application), which is an Airtable/phone-screen problem, not a page problem.
- **`/houses/cactus`** — 89.5% engagement rate, the highest of any page, on a single-CTA studio listing (`cta_email_cactus`). Short session (20.6s) is expected for a one-room, one-decision page — not a red flag here.
- **Organic Search (google, 103 sessions, 50.5% engagement)** — second-largest channel and reasonably engaged, consistent with STRATEGY.md's note that this is mostly competitor-name / generic-term traffic rather than ICP intent, but it isn't bouncing outright.

## What's underperforming

- **`/p/chores-as-complex-coordination`** — 51 sessions, 51 pageviews (1:1 — nobody navigates anywhere else on the site from it), 15.7% engagement rate despite a 74.6s avg duration. It's the third-biggest page on the site and a complete funnel dead end.
- **Direct channel bounce** — 180 sessions at 23.9% engagement is the biggest single leak in the data. Likely candidates: untagged links from Craigslist/SpareRoom/Listings Project listings (CO-GTM.md's off-site listing surfaces) or app-wrapped traffic that GA4 can't attribute, landing visitors on `/` instead of the page matching their intent.
- **`/chorewheel` → `/chorewheel/start`** — 22 engaged sessions on `/chorewheel`, only 1 `cta_cw_get_started` click and 7 sessions on `/chorewheel/start`. Visitors read the pitch but don't take the next step.
- **`/about`** — 31 sessions, 58.1% engagement, 123.7s avg duration (people read it), but zero `cta_book_consult` or `cta_email_about` events in 30 days. Either the CTAs aren't converting or tracking on this page is broken — worth ruling out the latter first.
